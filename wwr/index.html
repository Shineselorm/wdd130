Experience the Volta River

Discover the thrill of white water rafting on Ghana's most scenic river. Whether you are a first-time paddler or an experienced adventurer, our expert guides deliver safe, unforgettable trips for every skill level.

Why Raft With Us

Expert-Guided Adventures

Our certified river guides have years of experience on the Volta River. Every trip includes a safety briefing, quality equipment, and personalized attention so you can focus on the thrill of the ride.

Trips for Every Explorer

From gentle family floats to adrenaline-pumping gorge runs, we offer a range of trips designed for all ages and experience levels. Explore our full lineup and find the adventure that fits your group.

The Volta River watershed is one of West Africa's most breathtaking landscapes. Paddle through lush gorges, past historic bridges, and alongside wildlife you will not find anywhere else.

Memories That Last

Every trip ends with stories worth telling. Join hundreds of satisfied adventurers who have made Volta River Adventures their go-to destination for outdoor fun in Ghana.
